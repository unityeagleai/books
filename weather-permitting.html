a novel that asks your sky

Weather Permitting

There is a kiosk at the end of a pier, and a man who opens it every single day, because of something Ada said when she left. This book has six versions. The weather over your head right now decides which one you get. If it is raining on you, it rains on the pier.

It asks your location once, only to ask the sky, and keeps nothing. Refuse, and you may choose a sky by hand, but the honest version is the one that asks.
